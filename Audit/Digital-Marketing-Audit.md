# Digital Marketing Audit - Eternal Ltd. (Zomato)

## Introduction

Digital marketing plays a major role in Eternal's growth strategy. The company uses social media, content marketing, technology, and customer data to attract and retain users.

---

# 1. Social Media Marketing

## Strategy

Eternal uses social media platforms to create brand awareness and engage with customers.

Main platforms:
- Instagram
- X (Twitter)
- YouTube
- LinkedIn

## Strengths

- Creative and humorous content
- Strong brand personality
- Trend-based campaigns
- High audience engagement

## Improvement Opportunities

- More user-generated content campaigns
- More regional content
- Increased focus on sustainability messaging

---

# 2. Content Marketing

## Current Approach

Eternal creates content through:

- Food-related campaigns
- Memes
- Brand storytelling
- Festival campaigns
- Customer-focused communication

## Impact

This helps the brand:
- Stay relevant
- Build emotional connection
- Increase customer engagement

---

# 3. Mobile App Marketing

The Zomato app focuses on:

- Personalised recommendations
- Restaurant discovery
- Ratings and reviews
- Offers and notifications
- Easy ordering experience

## Strengths

- User-friendly interface
- Strong customer data usage
- Convenient ordering process

---

# 4. Search Engine Marketing (SEM) & SEO

Eternal benefits from:

- High search visibility
- Restaurant-related searches
- Food discovery content

Opportunities:
- More SEO-focused food guides
- Local search optimisation
- City-specific content

---

# 5. Customer Relationship Marketing

Eternal uses:

- Push notifications
- Personalised offers
- Customer feedback
- Loyalty programs

These strategies help increase repeat orders and customer retention.

---

# 6. SWOT - Digital Marketing Perspective

## Strengths
- Strong online presence
- Creative communication
- Large digital audience

## Weaknesses
- Dependence on digital platforms
- Customer loyalty affected by offers

## Opportunities
- AI-driven personalisation
- Influencer collaborations
- Community-based campaigns

## Threats
- Competitor marketing campaigns
- Rising advertising costs

---

# Recommendations

- Create more personalised marketing campaigns
- Increase influencer partnerships
- Build stronger loyalty programs
- Use AI for better customer targeting
- Focus on sustainable brand communication

---

# Conclusion

Eternal has developed a strong digital marketing presence through creative campaigns, technology, and customer engagement. Future growth will depend on deeper personalisation and stronger customer relationships.
