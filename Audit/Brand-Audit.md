# Brand Audit - Eternal Ltd. (Zomato)

## Brand Overview

Eternal Ltd. (formerly Zomato) is a technology-driven company operating in India's food-tech ecosystem. The brand connects customers with restaurants through food discovery, online ordering, and delivery services.

## Brand Positioning

Eternal positions itself as a convenient, technology-focused platform that helps customers discover food, order meals, and explore dining experiences.

The brand focuses on:
- Convenience
- Variety
- Customer experience
- Digital innovation

## Brand Identity

### Brand Personality
- Fun and relatable
- Youth-focused
- Innovative
- Customer-centric

### Brand Communication Style
Zomato is known for:
- Humorous marketing campaigns
- Simple and memorable messaging
- Social media engagement
- Real-time trend-based content

## Brand Strengths

- Strong brand recognition in India
- Large customer base
- Strong restaurant network
- High digital presence
- Data-driven customer insights

## Brand Challenges

- High competition from Swiggy and other platforms
- Customer loyalty influenced by discounts
- Need for continuous innovation
- Changing consumer expectations

## Brand Opportunities

- AI-based personalised recommendations
- Sustainable delivery solutions
- Premium dining experiences
- Stronger loyalty programs

## Conclusion

Eternal has successfully created a strong digital-first brand by combining technology, customer insights, and creative marketing. Continued innovation and customer-focused strategies can help strengthen its market position.
