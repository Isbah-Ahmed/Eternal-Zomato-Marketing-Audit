# Company Overview - Eternal Ltd. (Zomato)

## Introduction
Eternal Ltd. is an Indian technology company operating in the food delivery and commerce sector. The company was formerly known as Zomato and focuses on connecting customers, restaurants, and delivery partners through technology.

## Business Segments

### Zomato
- Food discovery and delivery platform
- Restaurant listings and customer reviews
- Online ordering services

### Blinkit
- Quick commerce platform
- Delivers groceries and daily essentials

### Hyperpure
- Supplies ingredients and products to restaurants

## Business Model

Eternal operates through:
- Commission from restaurant partners
- Delivery fees
- Advertising revenue
- Subscription-based services

## Target Customers

- Urban consumers
- Students and working professionals
- Food enthusiasts
- Customers looking for convenience

## Marketing Importance

Eternal has built a strong brand presence through:
- Digital campaigns
- Customer-focused technology
- Strong brand communication
