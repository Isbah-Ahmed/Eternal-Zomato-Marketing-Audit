# Customer Analysis - Eternal Ltd. (Zomato)

## Introduction

Understanding customer behaviour is essential for building effective marketing strategies. This analysis studies Eternal's (Zomato) target customers, their needs, buying behaviour, and decision-making factors.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Customers

- Young professionals
- College students
- Urban consumers
- Working individuals
- Food enthusiasts

### Demographic Profile

**Age Group:**
18-45 years

**Location:**
Major cities and urban areas

**Lifestyle:**
- Busy schedules
- Preference for convenience
- Interest in trying new food experiences

---

## Customer Needs

Eternal customers mainly look for:

- Quick and reliable food delivery
- Wide restaurant choices
- Easy ordering experience
- Reviews and ratings before purchase
- Discounts and offers
- Personalised recommendations

---

## Customer Buying Behaviour

### Awareness Stage
Customers discover restaurants and offers through:
- Social media
- Online advertisements
- Word of mouth
- App notifications

### Consideration Stage
Customers compare:
- Restaurant ratings
- Prices
- Delivery time
- Reviews
- Offers

### Purchase Stage
Customers place orders based on:
- Convenience
- Availability
- Delivery experience
- Payment options

### Post-Purchase Stage
Customer experience depends on:
- Food quality
- Delivery service
- Customer support
- Reviews and feedback

---

## Customer Segmentation

### 1. Convenience Seekers
Customers who order food because it saves time.

### 2. Food Explorers
Customers who enjoy discovering new restaurants and cuisines.

### 3. Discount-Oriented Customers
Customers influenced by offers and promotions.

### 4. Premium Customers
Customers interested in better dining experiences and exclusive services.

---

## Customer Pain Points

- Delivery delays
- High delivery charges
- Increasing food prices
- Inconsistent restaurant quality
- Too many similar choices making decisions difficult

---

## Marketing Opportunities

Eternal can improve customer engagement through:

- AI-based personalised food suggestions
- More loyalty rewards
- Better customer retention campaigns
- Health-focused food recommendations
- Personalised offers based on ordering patterns

---

## Conclusion

Eternal's success depends on understanding changing customer expectations. By using customer data and personalised marketing strategies, the brand can improve customer satisfaction and long-term loyalty.
