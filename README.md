# Eternal (Zomato) Marketing Audit 📊

A strategic marketing audit of Eternal Ltd. (formerly Zomato) analysing its brand positioning, customer behaviour, digital marketing strategy, competitors, and growth opportunities.

## 📌 Project Overview

This project examines Eternal's marketing approach and evaluates how the company builds customer relationships, creates brand value, and competes in India's food-tech ecosystem.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyse Eternal's brand positioning
- Understand customer segments and behaviour
- Evaluate digital marketing strategies
- Study competitors and market trends
- Provide marketing recommendations

## 🔍 Areas Covered

- Company Overview
- Brand Audit
- Customer Analysis
- Market Analysis
- Competitor Analysis
- Digital Marketing Audit
- SWOT Analysis
- Marketing Recommendations
