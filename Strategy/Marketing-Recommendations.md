# Marketing Recommendations - Eternal Ltd. (Zomato)

## Introduction

Based on the brand audit, customer analysis, market analysis, and competitor analysis, the following recommendations can help Eternal improve customer engagement, brand loyalty, and long-term growth.

---

# 1. Improve Customer Personalisation

## Recommendation

Use AI and customer data to provide more personalised experiences.

## Implementation

- Personalised food suggestions based on previous orders
- Recommendations based on location, time, and preferences
- Customised offers for different customer segments

## Expected Impact

- Higher customer satisfaction
- Increased repeat orders
- Better customer retention

---

# 2. Strengthen Customer Loyalty Programs

## Recommendation

Develop stronger loyalty strategies to reduce customer switching.

## Implementation

- Reward points system
- Exclusive benefits for frequent customers
- Special offers for loyal users

## Expected Impact

- Increased customer lifetime value
- Stronger brand loyalty

---

# 3. Create More Community-Based Campaigns

## Recommendation

Build stronger emotional connections with customers.

## Implementation

- Food stories from different cities
- Customer-generated content campaigns
- Local restaurant highlights

## Expected Impact

- Higher engagement
- Stronger brand relationship

---

# 4. Expand Sustainable Marketing

## Recommendation

Promote environmentally responsible practices.

## Implementation

- Eco-friendly packaging partnerships
- Sustainable restaurant recommendations
- Green delivery initiatives

## Expected Impact

- Improved brand image
- Attraction of environmentally conscious customers

---

# 5. Enhance Premium Food Experiences

## Recommendation

Create more premium offerings for customers seeking unique experiences.

## Implementation

- Exclusive restaurant partnerships
- Fine dining recommendations
- Special food events

## Expected Impact

- Higher customer spending
- Premium brand perception

---

# 6. Improve Regional Marketing

## Recommendation

Create more location-specific campaigns.

## Implementation

- Regional language content
- Local food promotions
- City-based marketing campaigns

## Expected Impact

- Better connection with local audiences
- Increased market reach

---

# 7. Focus on Gen Z Marketing

## Recommendation

Target younger audiences through creative digital strategies.

## Implementation

- Influencer collaborations
- Short-form video content
- Trend-based campaigns
- Campus marketing initiatives

## Expected Impact

- Increased brand engagement
- Stronger future customer base

---

# Conclusion

Eternal already has a strong market position due to its brand recognition, technology, and customer base. By focusing on personalisation, loyalty, sustainability, and community-driven marketing, the company can improve customer relationships and maintain long-term growth.
